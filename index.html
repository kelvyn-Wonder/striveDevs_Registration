frequently asked questions (FAQ)
What is this community about?

Our community is dedicated to helping students and budding developers from all skill levels learn and grow in the world of IT. Whether you’re just starting your coding journey or want to refine your full-stack skills, we provide resources, support, and collaboration opportunities to help you succeed.

Do I need to know how to code before joining?

No! Our community is perfect for beginners. We offer resources and mentorship for people with no coding knowledge. You can start with the basics and progress to advanced topics like full-stack development as you go.

What programming languages should I learn first?

We recommend starting with beginner-friendly languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Once you're comfortable, you can expand into back-end technologies like Node.js, Python, Ruby, or even Java to become a well-rounded full-stack developer.

Are there any events or meetups I can attend?

Yes! We host regular workshops, coding challenges, webinars, and meetups (virtual and in-person) to help you network with other developers and improve your skills.

Is there a membership fee or any hidden costs?

No, joining the community is completely free. Our mission is to make tech education accessible to everyone, so all resources and mentorship programs are free of charge.

Do you offer job placement or internships?

While we don't guarantee placements, we do collaborate with tech companies that occasionally offer internships and job opportunities to community members. Additionally, we provide career guidance, resume reviews, and interview preparation support.
